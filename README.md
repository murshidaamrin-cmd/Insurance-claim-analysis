# 🛡️ Insurance Claims Analysis Dashboard

> An end-to-end Data Analytics project built using **SQL** and **Power BI** to analyze insurance policy performance, claim trends, customer demographics, and business KPIs.

---

## 📌 Project Overview

Insurance companies handle thousands of policies and claims every day. Monitoring claim trends, policy performance, premium collections, and customer demographics is essential for reducing financial risk and improving operational efficiency.

This project demonstrates how raw insurance data can be transformed into meaningful business insights using **SQL** for data preparation and **Power BI** for interactive visualization.

---

## 🎯 Business Problem

Insurance companies often struggle to answer questions such as:

- Which policy types generate the highest premium?
- How many claims are pending, settled, or rejected?
- Which customer age group submits the highest claim amount?
- What is the distribution of active vs inactive policies?
- How much coverage has been provided compared to claims paid?

This dashboard helps business stakeholders answer these questions instantly.

# 📊 Dashboard Insights

Identifies which insurance products generate the highest premium revenue.

- Travel Insurance generates the highest premium.
- Home Insurance contributes the least premium.

## Claims by Claim Status

- Rejected Claims
- Settled Claims
- Pending Claims

## Active vs Inactive Policies

Shows policy distribution.

Useful for understanding customer retention and inactive policy percentage.

## Claim Amount by Age Group

## Gender Distribution

Displays customer distribution by gender.
